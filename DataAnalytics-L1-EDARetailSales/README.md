# 📊 Retail Sales EDA

This project is part of my **Oasis Infobyte Data Analytics Internship**.

The objective of this project is to perform **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** on a Retail Sales dataset to uncover customer purchasing behavior, sales trends, and business insights using Python.

---

## 🎯 Project Objective

- Understand the structure of the dataset
- Perform data cleaning and preprocessing
- Analyze customer demographics
- Explore product category performance
- Identify monthly and quarterly sales trends
- Generate business insights through visualizations

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Libraries

- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn
- Jupyter Notebook

---

## 📊 Analysis Performed

- ✅ Data Loading & Inspection
- ✅ Data Cleaning
- ✅ Missing Value Check
- ✅ Descriptive Statistics
- ✅ Monthly Sales Trend
- ✅ Quarterly Sales Trend
- ✅ Customer Age Group Analysis
- ✅ Gender-wise Sales Analysis
- ✅ Revenue by Product Category
- ✅ Correlation Heatmap
- ✅ Additional Business Visualization
- ✅ Business Recommendations

---

## 📂 Project Files

- `RetailSalesEDA.ipynb` – Complete Jupyter Notebook
- `retail_sales_dataset.csv` – Dataset used for analysis

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- Beauty and Clothing categories generated strong sales.
- Monthly sales showed noticeable fluctuations throughout the year.
- Customer purchasing behavior varies across age groups.
- Correlation analysis helped identify relationships between numerical features.
